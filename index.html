About Us

Videh signifies transcending beyond the body and mind.

Our training programs are crafted and delivered by a group of Certified Isha Hatha Yoga Teachers, meticulously trained under the guidance of Sadhguru. Immerse yourself in the purest form of yoga and meditations directly derived from the source, Adi Yogi (The Shiva), with our carefully curated programs.
